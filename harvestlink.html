Bridging Field & Community Kitchen

We founded HarvestLink to solve a critical inequality in the South African food system. Fresh produce should reach every community kitchen — not go to waste on farm.

🌍 Our Mission

Why HarvestLink Exists

While local farmers produce nutrient-rich harvests, community kitchens and schools often struggle to find consistent, affordable supplies. We act as the vital connector.

👨‍🌾

Empowering Local Farmers

We bridge the infrastructure gap for local growers, providing direct access to wholesale institutional markets and ensuring fair economic returns for their harvest.

🍽️

Supporting Food Security

Our marketplace prioritizes delivery to schools and community kitchens, ensuring fresh, local produce reaches the vulnerable populations that need it most.

📅

Fostering Seasonal Awareness

We educate our community on seasonal availability, helping organizations plan nutritional menus that celebrate local harvest while reducing our carbon footprint.
